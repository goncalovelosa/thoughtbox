## Sending Notes

- Send in batches of 10-15 per day (avoid spam filters)
- Personalize the first line if you can see what they work on from their GitHub profile
- Track replies in a simple spreadsheet: name, date sent, replied (y/n), feedback summary
- Follow up once after 7 days if no reply, then stop
- Never send to no-reply or organizational accounts
